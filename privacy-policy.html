Last updated: January 2026

LegalChain respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our mobile application and related services.

We may collect the following information when you use LegalChain:

Your information is used to:

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. Your information is only shared when required by law or to protect the safety and integrity of the LegalChain platform.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as your account is active or as required to provide our services and comply with legal obligations.

7. Children’s Privacy

LegalChain is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
