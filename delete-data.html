Request Data Deletion – LegalChain

Last updated: January 2026

At LegalChain, we respect your right to control your personal data. You may request deletion of your account and associated information at any time.

1. What Data Will Be Deleted

When your deletion request is approved, we will remove:

  • Your account information (name, email, phone number)
  • Login credentials
  • Professional details (for lawyers)
  • Uploaded documents

2. How to Request Deletion

Please send an email to:

legalchain.app@gmail.com

Include the following details in your email:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your full name
  • Your role (Client or Lawyer)

3. Processing Time

We will verify your request and delete your data within 7 business days.

4. Confirmation

Once your data is deleted, you will receive a confirmation email from our support team.
